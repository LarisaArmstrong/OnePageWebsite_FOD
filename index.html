Yellow Brick Road Travels is a travel corporation based in Norway. We offer unforgettable experiences to the youth between ages 18-30 years for great deals. At Yellow Brick Road you will be looked after by the provision of transport, accomodation and tours for single or multiple locations.

1 / Iceland

Iceland, a Nordic island nation, is defined by its dramatic landscape with volcanoes, geysers, hot springs and lava fields.

Featured Desitinations

Maelifell Volcano, Myrdalsjökull Glacier Park

The perfect cone makes Maelifell a classic looking volcano. During the warm season, snow uncovers a lavish green surface, covered with moss. There is plenty to do and see in the park, full of volcanoes, hot springs and other beautiful sites.
